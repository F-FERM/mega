Field to Report

From borehole to certified report

01 · Field

Geotechnical drilling & sampling

Our own crew drills boreholes by percussion or rotary coring per BS 5930, running Standard Penetration Tests, groundwater sampling and piezometer installation on site.

02 · Laboratory

Compressive & physical testing

Concrete cubes, cores and beams are cured and loaded on our Advanced CTM‑3000kN; aggregates, cement and steel undergo full physical and chemical testing to BS/ASTM.

03 · Quality Review

QA/QC verification

Every result is checked against our ISO/IEC 17025:2017 quality system before it leaves the lab — impartial, confidential, and traceable.

04 · Delivery

Certified test report

A signed, accredited report is issued referencing the exact standard applied — ready for submission to your consultant or authority.

Our Process

How a test moves through our lab

01

Sample collection & field investigation

Materials are sampled on site, or our team performs drilling, in‑situ density and plate load tests directly in the field.

02

Laboratory testing

Samples are logged and tested against the applicable BS or ASTM method — from sieve analysis to compressive strength.

03

QA/QC review

Results are cross‑checked by our QC Manager against the quality system before sign‑off.

04

Certified report issued

A signed report, referencing the exact standard, is delivered to your consultant or authority.

05

Consultation

Our engineers are available to walk through results, mix designs, or remedial recommendations.
